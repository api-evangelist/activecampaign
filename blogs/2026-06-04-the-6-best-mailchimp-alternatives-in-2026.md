Mailchimp remains popular for beginners but increasingly frustrates growing teams facing constraints in automation depth, segmentation flexibility, pricing structure, and support accessibility. The article identifies six alternatives - ActiveCampaign, Moosend, Brevo, Kit, AWeber, and GetResponse - each addressing specific pain points that prompt Mailchimp departures. Selection depends on business model, growth trajectory, and technical sophistication rather than feature checklists alone.
