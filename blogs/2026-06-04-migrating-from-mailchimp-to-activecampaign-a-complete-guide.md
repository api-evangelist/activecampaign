The article outlines how to transition from Mailchimp to ActiveCampaign using two approaches: an automated migration solution powered by Active Intelligence that transfers contacts, lists, tags, templates, and automations in hours, and detailed manual migration steps for users preferring greater control. Switching from one email marketing platform to another used to mean weeks of manual work, but the automated path dramatically reduces that burden. The guide covers both methods to help teams choose the approach that best fits their migration complexity and preferences.
